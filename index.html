How can I help you today?

I am an experimental digital artist based in London, United Kingdom. I use a variety of software and artificial intelligence to create my artwork.
Paying particular attention to textures, colours and meanings, I draw my inspiration from minimalism (*), good music, my own experiences, the animal kingdom, and few futuristic thoughts I have.
My works despict an invented reality that belongs to me and to those who wish to share it. My artwork is minted on the Algorand blockchain and can be purchased on EXA Market.

(*) Minimalism is the philosophy of achieving simplicity. Applied to design and artwork, minimalism includes removing what is unnecessary, has little use and distracts.
