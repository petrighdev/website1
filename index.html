Independent Security Consulting

I pioneer the development of efficient security systems and operations with my in-house methodology that is made of three steps.

Evaluate, Enhance, Endure®


  • Evaluate: I study how your security works in normal conditions, when stressed by threat vectors, when failing, and when recovering. I help you to understand what works efficiently and what is not. I define and reformulate problems, analyse variables, identify weaknesses, limitations and constraints, and then scope the improvements that should be done;
  • Enhance: based on the findings of the previous step, I conceptualise a set of solutions at strategical, tactical and operational levels, and assist your organisation during the implementation phases as appropriate; the solutions I develop are bespoke;
  • Endure: I review and evaluate the solutions implemented during the second step, and ensure that your organisation benefits from continuous security improvements through quality and assurance processes, auditing, training and certifications.

My work includes qualitative and quantitative research; various assessments (website and email security, processes, operations, information security, human factors); development of policies, procedures, methods, frameworks, actions plans and tools; audits; reviews; and testing. All the work I do is proprietary, meaning it belongs solely to my clients (I am not sharing anything, and I use strict control mechanisms to protect information and maintain confidentiality).
I am a problem solver thinking outside the box, and my work is innovative and somewhat unorthodox. If you are looking for something new that will help you solve your security problems, then I am probably a good fit.

A performing and secure website


Website performance is something rather rare in today's world, and it is as such for many reasons. Having a performing and secure website has many advantages compared to having a "normal website". For example, a performing website allows you to stand out from the crowd, requires almost no computing power, consumes less energy, loads faster, is more stable and secure; it expresses a way of doing things, a way of being. Performing websites also enhance the overall experience of websites' users, including their own security.
(*) Minimalism is the philosophy of achieving simplicity. Applied to website design, minimalism includes removing what is unnecessary, has little use and distracts.
