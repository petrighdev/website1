A performing and secure website


Website performance is something rather rare in today's world, and it is as such for many reasons. Having a performing and secure website has many advantages compared to having a "normal website". For example, a performing website allows you to stand out from the crowd, requires almost no computing power, consumes less energy, loads faster, is more stable and secure; it expresses a way of doing things, a way of being. Performing websites also enhance the overall experience of websites' users, including their own security.
(*) Minimalism is the philosophy of achieving simplicity. Applied to website design, minimalism includes removing what is unnecessary, has little use and distracts.
