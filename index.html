Website design

I like to design websites that fit in a single HTML document (1 page in total), are weighting less than 10Kb, and that make 1 or 2 HTTP requests to fully load and be functional, similarly to my own website.
The websites I design have a valid source code without any coding error or warning, have embedded security measures to prevent hacking, and have no cookie so as to preserve privacy. I can either create bespoke websites, or replicate my own website by making minor adjustments such as replacing the text, changing colours and logo / picture.

A simple website


Simplicity is something rather rare in today's world, and it is not that simple to do things simply. Simple, text-based websites have many advantages compared to bloated and regular websites. For example, they allow you to stand out from the crowd, require almost no computing power, consume less energy, load faster, are stable and clear; they express a way of doing things, a lifestyle. Simple websites can be used in many ways as well, for example as an online CV, a corporate website, a blog, or a place to organise and share your thoughts with the world.
(*) Minimalism is the philosophy of achieving simplicity. Applied to website design, minimalism includes removing what is unnecessary, has little use and distract.
